"Logic and Computing" is one of the special sessions of the first joint meeting of the Korean Mathematical Society and the German Mathematical Society. The purpose of this meeting is to enhance and encourage the scientific collaboration between the German and Korean mathematical communities in all areas of mathematical sciences. All scientific sessions and events will take place at Coex in Seoul, Korea from October 3 to 6, 2018. Please visit HERE for more information about KMS-DMV Joint Meeting.

Our session will be held at Slot A(9:00-10:00, Oct. 04), Slot B(13:30-15:30, Oct. 04), and Slot E(13:30-15:30, Oct. 05).
